Platform

Built for clinical seriousness.

Every design decision favours clarity, safety, and dependability.

Structured Triage

Every response includes a clear urgency level — Emergency, See a Doctor, or Home Care — never ambiguous.

Safety-First Guardrails

Emergency situations are flagged immediately. No prescriptions, no dosages — responsible by design.

Always-On Reliability

If the main assessment engine is ever busy, the system automatically continues with its own built-in medical knowledge base — you are never left waiting.

Smart Case Memory

The system recognises cases it has already assessed and responds instantly, keeping the service quick and consistent.

Context-Aware Follow-up

The assistant remembers your conversation and asks targeted follow-up questions to refine its assessment.

Confidential by Design

Conversations are used only to generate and recall assessments. No personally identifying information is required to use the service.

How It Works

Three steps. Under a minute.

  1. 1

    Describe your symptoms

    Type what you're feeling — onset, location, severity, and any relevant history. The more detail, the sharper the assessment.

  2. 2

    The assistant asks follow-up questions

    If anything is unclear, you'll be asked targeted follow-up questions before an assessment is formed — just like a triage nurse would.

  3. 3

    Receive a structured report

    You'll get an urgency level, the most likely conditions explained in plain language, and clear guidance on what to do next.

About the Project

An academic exploration in accessible health guidance.

This system was developed as a final year research project to explore how a layered, fault-tolerant design can keep a health-information assistant dependable — combining a modern reasoning engine with a transparent, rule-based knowledge base and a memory of previously assessed cases.

It is intended purely for education and preliminary guidance. It does not replace consultation, examination, or treatment by a qualified physician.
